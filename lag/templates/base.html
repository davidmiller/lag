Welcome to LAG, stranger

LAG, the Location-Aware Game (also Locations, Artifacts, Gamers), is a multiverse of people, swag and activity whose core interactions depend upon your real life location. Discover and create places in LAG that map to places in the physical world around you, find exotic artifacts, and interact with other gamers peopling the LAG universe to dictate and participate in adventurous narratives for the sake of action, curiosity, wanderlust, profit, mischievousness or pure malicious usurpation & dominance!

Make yourself known. Register to create a new persona, or log in to resume your existing persona's activities.
